FAU Facts

  • FAU current President: Mary Jane Saunders

  • The FAU Mascot is the Burrowing Owl

    His name is Owlsey

  • FAU currently has 6 Dorm buildings

    IVA, UVA, IRT, GPT, HPT, and Algonquin

  • Florida Atlantic University, the fifth public university in Florida, opened its doors in 1964

    with an enrollment of 867 upper-division and graduate students.

  • FAU currently has six satellite campuses

    Located in Dania Beach, Davie, Fort Lauderdale, Jupiter, Port St. Lucie, and Fort Pierce.

  • The undergraduate student body contains 44% ethnic minorities

    and includes students from 140 countries, 49 states, and the District of Columbia
